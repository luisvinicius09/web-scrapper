This page contains a list of all known tasks in the Trailblazer Reloaded League. Completing tasks during the league allows players to earn league points and unlock relics that buff their character and areas containing new bosses and tasks. Tasks span five difficulty levels - Easy, Medium, Hard, Elite, and Master.
